Hotel Paradise Resort Information

Check-in and Check-out:
- Check-in time: 3:00 PM
- Check-out time: 11:00 AM
- Early check-in and late check-out available upon request (fees may apply)
- Photo ID and credit card required at check-in
- Express check-out available through the hotel app or TV system

Room Information:
- All rooms feature air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and free high-speed Wi-Fi
- Deluxe rooms include a balcony with ocean view
- Suites include a separate living area and kitchenette
- Accessible rooms available upon request
- All rooms have a mini-fridge, coffee maker, and in-room safe
- Cribs and rollaway beds available upon request (fees may apply for rollaway beds)

Dining Options:
- The Ocean View Restaurant: Open 7:00 AM - 10:00 PM, serving international cuisine
- Poolside Bar: Open 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM, serving drinks and light snacks
- Room service available 24/7
- The Rooftop Lounge: Open 5:00 PM - 12:00 AM, serving cocktails and tapas
- Breakfast buffet served daily from 7:00 AM - 10:30 AM ($25 per person)

Facilities:
- Outdoor swimming pool: Open 7:00 AM - 9:00 PM
- Fitness center: Open 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM
- Spa: Open 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM (reservations recommended)
- Business center: Open 24/7
- Gift shop: Open 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- Concierge desk: Open 7:00 AM - 11:00 PM

Spa Services:
- Full-service spa offering massages, facials, and body treatments
- Couples massage packages available
- Steam room and sauna included with any spa treatment
- Advanced booking recommended
- 24-hour cancellation policy applies

Transportation:
- Airport shuttle service available (reservation required, $30 each way)
- Valet parking: $35 per night with in/out privileges
- Self-parking: $25 per night with in/out privileges
- Car rental desk in lobby: Open 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Taxi stand at main entrance

Policies:
- No smoking in rooms (designated smoking areas available)
- Pets allowed in designated pet-friendly rooms (maximum 2 pets, $50 fee per stay)
- Cancellation policy: 48 hours notice required to avoid charges
- Quiet hours: 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM
- Maximum occupancy: 4 persons per standard room

Internet Access:
- Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the property
- Premium high-speed option available for streaming ($15 per day)
- Business center computers available for guest use
- Wi-Fi password provided at check-in

Local Attractions:
- Beach access: Direct access from hotel (beach towels provided)
- Shopping district: 5-minute walk
- Museum of Modern Art: 10-minute drive
- National Park: 20-minute drive
- Golf course: 15-minute drive (special rates for hotel guests)

Emergency Information:
- Emergency exits are located on each floor
- First aid kit available at the front desk
- Nearest hospital: City General Hospital (5-minute drive)
- In case of emergency, dial 0 from your room phone or contact the front desk
